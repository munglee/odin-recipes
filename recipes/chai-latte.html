Chai Latte

Description

A chai latte is a flavorful, spiced tea combined with steamed milk. It originated from India as "masala chai" (spiced tea) and became popular in cafes worldwide, especially in Western countries.

Ingredients

  • Chai tea concentrate (or black tea with chai spices like cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, ginger, and black pepper)
  • Steamed milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Sweetener (sugar, honey, or maple syrup - optional)

Steps

  1. Brew the chai concentrate: Use a chai tea bag or brew black tea with chai spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, ginger, etc.) in hot water. Let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Steam the milk: Heat milk (or your preferred non-dairy milk) and steam it until frothy.
  3. Combine: Pour the chai concentrate into a cup and add steamed milk.
  4. Sweeten: Stir in your choice of sweetener.
  5. Serve: Top with extra cinnamon or nutmeg if desired, and enjoy!

Enjoy your aromatic, spiced chai latte!
