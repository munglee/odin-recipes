Hot Chocolate

Description

A hot chocolate is a rich and comforting beverage made with melted chocolate and milk, perfect for chilly days. It's creamy and indulgent, often topped with whipped cream or marshmallows.

Ingredients

  • Milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate (chips, chopped, or melted)
  • Sugar (optional, depending on chocolate used)
  • Vanilla extract (optional for extra flavor)
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the milk: In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until warm but not boiling.
  2. Add cocoa or chocolate: Stir in cocoa powder or chopped chocolate until fully dissolved and smooth.
  3. Sweeten: Add sugar, if needed, to taste, and stir until well combined.
  4. Flavor it: Add a splash of vanilla extract if desired for extra flavor.
  5. Serve: Pour the hot chocolate into a mug and top with whipped cream, marshmallows, or a sprinkle of cocoa powder if desired.

Enjoy your cozy, creamy hot chocolate!
